Hardware & enablement

Buy a proper data collection setup—we build it around your robot

Beyond datasets alone: purchase a complete capture program from RoboDIP. We specify hardware for your embodiment, integrate sensors and edge loggers, stand up ROS 2 recording and time sync, train your team, and hand off runbooks—so you collect train-ready data reliably, not a shelf of parts.

What you buy

  • Curated stacks—arms, grippers, RGB-D / multi-cam, tactile or F/T, teleop (VR, SpaceMouse), Jetson or IPC loggers—matched to your robot class and task
  • Lab bench, pilot line, or fleet-ready configurations with a clear BOM and sourcing
  • Optional upgrades: mobile bases, additional viewpoints, factory-hardened enclosures

What we deliver

  • Integration & setup — mechanical fit-up, cabling, network and storage, sensor calibration, clock sync, topic naming aligned to your URDF / fleet
  • Software bring-up — ROS 2 logging profiles, MCAP/bag defaults, QA hooks tied to our processing layer
  • Training & handover — operator and maintainer sessions, runbooks, documentation
  • Support — remote or on-site commissioning available; follow-on help under contract
